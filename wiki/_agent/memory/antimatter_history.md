## Dirac Equation — Core Points

- **Problem:** Schrödinger's equation was non-relativistic; Klein-Gordon had negative probabilities
- **Solution:** Linearized Hamiltonian using 4×4 gamma matrices → 4-component spinor
- **Four solutions:** two positive-energy (electron spin-up/down) + two negative-energy (positron spin-up/down)
- **Emergent predictions:** electron spin-1/2 and g=2 fall out automatically; antiparticles required
- **Equation:** `(iγ^μ ∂_μ - mc/ħ)ψ = 0`

## Anderson's Cloud Chamber Experiment — Key Details

- Lead plate (1.5 cm) bisecting chamber = direction-of-travel disambiguator
- Magnetic field ~2.4 T; curvature below plate (r≈23 cm) vs above (r≈5 cm) → particle going upward
- Curvature + ionization density → electron mass, positive charge → **positron**
- Published: _Physical Review_ 43, 491 (1933)

## Baryon Asymmetry — Core Problem

- Universe is ~100% matter; Big Bang should have made equal matter+antimatter
- Observed: η = nB/nγ ≈ 6×10⁻¹⁰ (1 baryon per 10 billion photons)
- Standard Model CP violation predicts η ~ 10⁻²⁰ — **10 orders of magnitude too small**
- Leading solution: **Leptogenesis** (heavy Majorana neutrino decays → lepton asymmetry → baryon asymmetry via sphalerons)

### Sakharov's 3 Conditions (1967)

1. Baryon number violation (ΔB ≠ 0)
2. C and CP symmetry violated
3. Thermal non-equilibrium

## Antihydrogen Spectroscopy Results (ALPHA at CERN)

- 1S–2S transition: matches hydrogen to **2×10⁻¹⁰** (2017)
- Electric charge: 0 within 7×10⁻¹⁰ e (2020)
- Gravity: falls at g ≈ 9.8 m/s² downward (2023) — no anti-gravity
- These results confirm CPT symmetry at very high precision
