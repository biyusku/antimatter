# Antimatter Applications — Medical & Future

Research area 4 of the Anti Madde documentary project. Covers practical and speculative uses of antimatter — from routine hospital scans to century-scale energy visions.

---

## PET Scanning — Key Facts

**What it is:** Positron Emission Tomography. The most common real-world use of antimatter.

**The tracer:** FDG (¹⁸F-fluorodeoxyglucose) — glucose with radioactive fluorine-18 substituted in. Cancer cells devour glucose; FDG gets trapped inside them and "lights up" on the scan.

**F-18 properties:**

- Half-life: **109.77 minutes** — short enough for same-day dosing, long enough to ship regionally
- Decay: β⁺ emission (positron = antimatter electron) → ¹⁸O
- Produced by cyclotron bombardment of O-18 enriched water

**Antimatter quantity per scan:**

- Injected dose: ~370 MBq (~10 mCi) of F-18
- F-18 atoms at injection: ~3.5 × 10¹² (~0.1 micrograms of material)
- Positron (antimatter) mass equivalent: ~**3 nanograms** — trillionths of a gram

**Physics of detection:**

- Each positron travels ~1–2 mm in tissue, stops, annihilates with an electron
- Produces two **511 keV** gamma rays flying in **exactly opposite directions (180°)**
- PET ring detectors use **coincidence detection** (~10 ns window) to define a Line of Response
- Millions of LORs reconstructed into 3D metabolic map

**Scale:** ~3–4 million PET scans/year (USA); ~8–12 million globally; ~10,000–15,000 scanners worldwide.

**Frontier — uEXPLORER (UC Davis, 2018):** 194 cm axial field covers whole body simultaneously. 40× greater sensitivity. Enables dynamic whole-body tracer kinetics imaging.

## Antiproton Cancer Therapy

**CERN ACE Experiment (2003–2013):** Tested antiproton irradiation of cancer cells at CERN's Antiproton Decelerator (AD).

**The annihilation bonus:** At the Bragg peak, antiproton annihilates with a nuclear proton → shower of pions and nuclear fragments precisely at the tumor. RBE = **4–5×** vs protons (~1.1×).

**Clinical status: NOT available.** No human trials. Barriers: needs CERN-scale infrastructure; carbon ion therapy already fills the high-LET clinical niche.

## Antihydrogen Science — ALPHA at CERN

**CPT Symmetry test:** Measure antihydrogen's 1S-2S transition vs hydrogen.

- Result (2022–2023): Agreement to **~12 parts per trillion** — no CPT violation detected.

**Gravity test — ALPHA-g (Nature, September 2023):**

- First direct measurement of gravity on antimatter
- Result: Antihydrogen **falls DOWN** — gravity acts same on matter and antimatter
- g_anti / g_matter = 1.00 ± 0.30
- Rules out "antigravity" as explanation for matter-antimatter asymmetry

## Baryon Asymmetry

**The mystery:** ~1 extra baryon per 10⁹ pairs survived the Big Bang — that surplus is everything that exists.

**Sakharov's 3 conditions (1967):** Baryon number violation + CP violation + departure from equilibrium. Standard Model satisfies all three but is ~10 orders of magnitude too weak.

**Leading solution:** Leptogenesis via heavy right-handed neutrinos. Active experiments: T2K, NOvA, DUNE (neutrino CP violation); LHCb, Belle II (B/D meson CP violation).

## Antimatter Power Generation

**Energy density:** 9 × 10¹³ MJ/kg — 2 billion × gasoline. To power a 1M-person city for 1 year: ~9 kg antimatter (theoretical).

**Why it's centuries away:** Current production ~nanograms/year globally; cost ~$62.5 trillion/gram; energy return ratio ~10⁻¹⁰. No viable path to the needed 10+ order-of-magnitude improvement.

## Future Production: Laser Pair Production

**Breit-Wheeler process:** γ + γ → e⁻ + e⁺. Needs laser intensity ~10²³ W/cm² (current max ~10²¹).
Key projects: **ELI Beamlines** (Europe), **LUXE at DESY** (Hamburg, results 2025–2028).
